How to Activate Microsoft Office

A complete walkthrough to activate your Office license using Microsoft's official activation tool — quick, easy, and takes just a few minutes.

5 min read Updated 2025 By KeyUpNow Team

In This Guide

  1. Open Word & Launch the Activation Wizard
  2. Go to the Microsoft Activation Tool
  3. Sign In to Your Microsoft Account
  4. Select "Activate a Microsoft Product"
  5. Choose Microsoft Office & Your OS
  6. Select Your Office Version
  7. Copy & Enter Your Installation ID
  8. Enter the Confirmation IDs
  9. Complete the Activation

Microsoft's official activation tool is one of the most reliable ways to activate your Office license. The process is simple, takes just a few minutes, and works for all Office versions. Follow the steps below to get your Office up and running.

Works With

Office 2024 Home & Business
Office 2021 Professional Plus
Office 2019 All Editions
Office 2016 All Editions
1
Open Word & Launch the Activation Wizard

Open Microsoft Word. The Activation Wizard pop-up should appear automatically. Select the option that says "I want to activate the software by telephone", then click Next.

Wizard not appearing? Open Word, go to Account (bottom-left), then click "Activate Product" on the red banner under Product Information.
Select "I want to activate the software by telephone" and click Next
Important — Please Read

You do NOT need to call Microsoft! Microsoft has discontinued phone activation. Instead, simply visit the official activation tool at https://aka.ms/aoh and follow the on-screen steps to complete your activation online. It's fast, free, and works 24/7.

2
Go to the Microsoft Activation Tool

Use Microsoft's official online activation tool to complete your activation:

Open Microsoft Activation Tool — aka.ms/aoh

Click "Let's Get Started" to begin the process.

Click "Let's Get Started" on the Microsoft Activation Tool
3
Sign In to Your Microsoft Account

You'll be asked to sign in with your Microsoft account. Use any Microsoft account — it doesn't need to match the one tied to Office. If you don't have one, you can create a free account.

Enter your Microsoft account email address
Enter your password to complete sign-in
4
Select "Activate a Microsoft Product"

After signing in, you'll see a few options. Choose "Activate a Microsoft Product" to proceed.

Select "Activate a Microsoft Product"
5
Choose Microsoft Office & Your Operating System

Select Microsoft Office as the product you want to activate. Then pick your operating system — typically Windows.

Select Microsoft Office and your operating system (Windows)
6
Select Your Office Version

Choose your specific Office version from the list (e.g., Office 2024, Office 2021, etc.). You'll then see a field where you need to enter your Installation ID.

Select your Office version — e.g. Office 2024
7
Copy & Enter Your Installation ID

Go back to the Activation Wizard in your Office application. You'll see a long series of numbers — this is your Installation ID. Copy all the number groups.

Copy the Installation ID from the Activation Wizard (Step 2)

Now paste these numbers into the corresponding fields on the Microsoft activation page.

Enter the Installation ID on the Microsoft activation tool
Important: Make sure you enter all the number groups correctly. A single wrong digit will generate an invalid Confirmation ID.
8
Enter the Confirmation IDs

After submitting your Installation ID, Microsoft will generate a set of Confirmation IDs. Copy them from the activation page.

Microsoft generates your Confirmation IDs — copy these numbers

Now go back to the Activation Wizard in Office and enter these IDs into the boxes in Step 3 of the wizard.

Paste the Confirmation IDs into Step 3 of the Activation Wizard
9
Complete the Activation

Click "Next" and you're done! Your Microsoft Office is now activated and ready to use. You should see a confirmation message saying the activation was successful.

You're all set! Your Office license is now permanently activated on this device. No subscription required — it's yours to keep.
